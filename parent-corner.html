Give your child the gift of Language Exchange

12 percent of Americans are Spanish speakers. Helping cultivate your child's ability to communicate with speakers of America's most common non-English language is a valuable gift that they will utilize their entire life.

Why Choose Us

Compassion

We model a moral compass of compassion and goodwill to instill gratitude and kindness in your child.

Diversity

Diversity is embraced and reflected in our teachings, books, and activities so your child grows into a world citizen.

Arts and Crafts

Arts are an avenue toward creative self-expression that strengthen motor skills and spur smiles.

Little Yellow House

Our schools have a “yellow house” stocked with all they need for dramatic play, so they can bring their own world to life.

Music and Dancing

Music and movement teach children to keep the beat with homemade instruments and exercise for physical health.

Well-Rounded

Whole child education prepares them for kindergarten because we address academic, social, creative, & practical skills.

Kindness

We overcome challenging behaviour with redirection and validation, making them feel safe and loved.

Expert Teachers

Our teachers are a core part of our commitment to children, and their expertise and nurturing guide them toward success.
